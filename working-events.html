What I Learned Working Big Events

My experience as an event usher and coordinator in Bahrain

Posted on March 10, 2024 5 min read Experience

Before diving fully into cybersecurity, I gained real-world experience working in high-profile events as an usher and coordinator for companies like Red Scarf, Gallure, and PICO Bahrain.

I was part of events such as the Gulf Gateway Summit, Moda Mall National Day, the Gastronomy Festival, and concerts, where I managed VIP guest logistics, guided attendees, and performed data entry tasks.

These experiences taught me how to work under pressure, communicate effectively with international teams, and deliver excellent service in front-facing roles. Today, these soft skills continue to support me in every tech and team-based challenge I take on.
